#+TITLE: OrgSync Privacy Policy

/Last updated: July 20, 2026/

OrgSync is built to be private by default. This policy describes what the app
does — and deliberately does not do — with your information.

* The short version

- OrgSync has *no accounts*, no analytics, no advertising, no telemetry, and no
  OrgSync-operated server.
- Your notes live on your device (or in your own iCloud Drive, GitHub
  repository, Reminders, or Calendar if you enable those integrations).
- We do not collect, track, analyze, sell, or share any personal data.
- Subscriptions are processed anonymously: the only identifier attached to a
  purchase is a random ID generated by RevenueCat.

* Data stored on your device

All app data is stored locally:

- *Notes* (=.org= files) are stored in the app's Documents directory. If you
  enable *Store Notes in iCloud Drive* in Settings, they are stored in the
  app's iCloud Drive folder instead, and Apple syncs them across your devices
  under Apple's own privacy terms.
- *Preferences and widget snapshots* are stored locally in the app's App Group
  container.
- *Your GitHub Personal Access Token* (if you connect a repository) is stored
  in the device Keychain and never leaves your device except to authenticate
  directly with GitHub.

OrgSync sends no data anywhere unless you explicitly enable one of the
optional integrations below.

* Optional integrations

Each of these is off until you turn it on, and each exchanges data only with
the service you choose:

- *GitHub sync* — note content is sent to and received from the GitHub
  repository you connect, using your own Personal Access Token, directly
  between your device and GitHub. GitHub's privacy policy applies to data
  stored there.
- *Reminders sync* — the app reads and writes the single Reminders list you
  choose, through Apple's EventKit APIs, entirely on your device.
- *Calendar sync* — the app reads events from your calendars through Apple's
  EventKit APIs and mirrors them into a local, read-only =calendar.org= file.
  Nothing is written back to your calendars.
- *iCloud Drive storage* — your notes folder is stored in the app's iCloud
  Drive container and synced by Apple.
- *Local notifications* — reminders for scheduled and deadline TODOs are
  generated and delivered entirely on your device. Note content used in a
  notification never leaves the device.

* Subscriptions and RevenueCat

The optional *OrgSync Pro* subscription is processed by Apple's App Store and
managed through RevenueCat, our subscription infrastructure provider.

- OrgSync has no account system, so there is nothing to link a purchase to:
  no name, no email address, and no note content is ever shared with
  RevenueCat.
- RevenueCat generates a *random, anonymous identifier* on your device and
  receives purchase receipt information from Apple to validate your
  subscription. That is the only identifier associated with your purchase.
- RevenueCat's handling of this data is described in their privacy policy:
  https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy
- Apple's processing of your payment is governed by Apple's own terms and
  privacy policy. We never see your payment details.

* What we collect

Nothing. OrgSync contains no analytics SDK and operates no server. We cannot
see your notes, your usage, your identity, or your device.

* Data retention and deletion

Because we hold no data, there is nothing for us to retain or delete. To
remove all app data, delete the app (and, if you enabled them, the notes
folder in iCloud Drive, your GitHub repository copy, and the Reminders list —
those are in your own accounts and under your control).

* Children's privacy

OrgSync does not collect data from anyone, including children.

* Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published at this URL with
a new "last updated" date. Because the app collects nothing, changes are
expected to be rare and editorial.
